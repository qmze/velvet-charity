About Us

Velvet Charity is dedicated to combating homelessness and improving community well-being through targeted street cleanup initiatives. Our approach involves direct outreach, transitional housing programs, and employment assistance, addressing the immediate needs of those experiencing homelessness while also creating sustainable pathways to stability. By fostering community partnerships and organizing regular clean-up campaigns, Velvet Charity strives to make a lasting impact, promoting a safer and more supportive environment for everyone.

Our Story

Inspired by the visible homelessness crisis on the streets of England, Velvet Charity was founded as a non-profit organization committed to making a positive change. Witnessing the pressing need for assistance, the organization initiated street cleanup programs and outreach efforts to address homelessness directly. This philanthropic journey led to the creation of Velvet Watches, a social enterprise born out of the charity's mission, dedicated to funding and supporting initiatives aimed at cleaning up the streets and providing sustainable solutions for those in need.

Our Achivments

In our brief existence, Velvet Charity has achieved significant milestones, with the most notable accomplishment being the assistance provided to 100 homeless individuals. Through our dedicated efforts, we have successfully implemented a range of initiatives aimed at tackling homelessness and creating a positive impact in our community. These achievements underscore our commitment to making a meaningful difference in the lives of those in need.
